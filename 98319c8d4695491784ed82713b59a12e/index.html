The staff and owners of Backyard BBQ and charleston Catering would like to serve your every need. We realize that our most important factors is our customers, which means you! We cater everything from the simple to the simply elegant. Our approach to your event is one of personal attention with a professional attitude. Embracing the opportunity to expand with the times, Charleston Catering is committed to the value that has produced success in a small town with nutritious and great tasting food, reasonable prices and superb service.


We have helped hundreds of clients plan the perfect occasion from Weddings, Corporate Catering, Special Events, Barbecues and Private Parties. We also offer a full line of service including pick-up or delivery, china or plastic utensils. Our buffets offer everything from hot dogs and barbecue to shrimp kabobs and prime rib hand carved to your satisfaction. You can choose from an assortment of hot or cold Hors d’oeuvre, variety of chips and dips, fresh fruit or meat trays. Our homemade desserts and gourmet cookies are second to none. We offer service for 50 to 5000.


As you plan that special event please let Charleston Catering turn the ordinary into an extraordinary event your friends and family will talk about for years to come.



We look forward to hearing from you soon!
